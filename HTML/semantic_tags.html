What is Panipuri?

Pani Puri is a beloved Indian street food snack featuring crispy, hollow, deep-fried dough balls (puris) filled with a flavorful mix of spiced potatoes, chickpeas, tamarind chutney, and spicy, tangy flavored water (pani), eaten whole in one bite for a burst of sour and spicy flavors. It's a popular chaat (savory snack) across the Indian subcontinent, with variations in fillings and water flavorings.

Did You Know?

Panipuri is also known as Golgappa in Delhi and Phuchka in West Bengal.
